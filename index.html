ABOUT

In a parallel universe, the ape civilisation finds itself abandoning its destroyed motherland, barren and with no prospect of survival. The apes escape on board their spaceship in search of new prospects for colonising the galaxy. They discover that they are not the only living beings navigating in space. They come up against the Aliens, dangerous subjects stronger than themselves, who threaten them using their dominance. They discover the $GALAX token and realise that it is the only tool they have left to survive and re-establish a civilisation.
